 79  /- Binder elimination:
 80  
 81  We assume a binder `B : p → Π (α : Sort u), (α → t) → t`, where `t` is a type depending on `p`.
 82  Examples:
 83    ∃: there is no `p` and `t` is `Prop`.
 84    ⨅, ⨆: here p is `β` and `[complete_lattice β]`, `p` is `β`
 85  
 86  Problem: ∀x, _ should be a binder, but is not a constant!
 87  
 88  Provide a mechanism to rewrite:
 89  
 90    B (x : α) ..x.. (h : x = t), p x  =  B ..x/t.., p t
 91  
 92  Here ..x.. are binders, maybe also some constants which provide commutativity rules with `B`.
 93  
 94  -/
