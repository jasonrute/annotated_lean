 10  # Bitraversable Lemmas
 11  
 12  ## Main definitions
 13    * tfst - traverse on first functor argument
 14    * tsnd - traverse on second functor argument
 15  
 16  ## Lemmas
 17  
 18  Combination of
 19    * bitraverse
 20    * tfst
 21    * tsnd
 22  
 23  with the applicatives `id` and `comp`
