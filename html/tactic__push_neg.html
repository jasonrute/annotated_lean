149  /--

151  `contrapose`     turns a goal `P → Q` into `¬ Q → ¬ P`
152  `contrapose!`    turns a goal `P → Q` into `¬ Q → ¬ P` and pushes negations inside `P` and `Q`

155  `contrapose! h`  first reverts the local assumption `h`, and then uses `contrapose!` and `intro h`
156  `contrapose h with new_h` uses the name `new_h` for the introduced hypothesis.
