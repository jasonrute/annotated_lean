 11  /--
 12  `restate_axiom` takes a structure field, and makes a new, definitionally simplified copy of it.
 13  If the existing field name ends with a `'`, the new field just has the prime removed. Otherwise,
 14  we append `_lemma`.
 15  The main application is to provide clean versions of structure fields that have been tagged with an auto_param.
 16  -/
