 23  `functor C D` represents a functor between categories `C` and `D`.
 24  
 25  To apply a functor `F` to an object use `F.obj X`, and to a morphism use `F.map f`.
 26  
 27  The axiom `map_id_lemma` expresses preservation of identities, and
 28  `map_comp_lemma` expresses functoriality.
