 17  /--
 18  `functor.category C D` gives the category structure on functors and natural transformations
 19  between categories `C` and `D`.
 20  
 21  Notice that if `C` and `D` are both small categories at the same universe level,
 22  this is another small category at that level.
 23  However if `C` and `D` are both large categories at the same universe level,
 24  this is a small category at the next higher level.
 25  -/
