 12  # Premetric spaces
 13  
 14  Metric spaces are often defined as quotients of spaces endowed with a "distance"
 15  function satisfying the triangular inequality, but for which `dist x y = 0` does
 16  not imply x = y. We call such a space a premetric space.
 17  `dist x y = 0` defines an equivalence relation, and the quotient
 18  is canonically a metric space.
 19  -/
