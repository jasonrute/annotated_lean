 10  # Contracting maps
 11  
 12  A Lipschitz continuous self-map with Lipschitz constant `K < 1` is called a *contracting map*.
 13  In this file we prove the Banach fixed point theorem, some explicit estimates on the rate
 14  of convergence, and some properties of the map sending a contracting map to its fixed point.
 15  -/
