 50  /-- In a goal of the form `tfae [a₀, a₁, a₂]`,
 51  `tfae_have : i → j` creates the assertion `aᵢ → aⱼ`. The other possible
 52  notations are `tfae_have : i ← j` and `tfae_have : i ↔ j`. The user can
 53  also provide a label for the assertion, as with `have`: `tfae_have h : i ↔ j`.
