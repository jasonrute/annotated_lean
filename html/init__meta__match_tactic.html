 22  /-- `mk_pattern ls es t u o` creates a new pattern with (length ls) universe meta-variables and (length es) meta-variables.
 23     In the produced pattern p, we have that
 24     - `pattern.target p` is the term t where the universes ls and expressions es have been replaced with temporary meta-variables.
 25     - `pattern.uoutput p` is the list u where the universes ls have been replaced with temporary meta-variables.
 26     - `pattern.moutput p` is the list o where the universes ls and expressions es have been replaced with temporary meta-variables.
 27     - `pattern.nuvars p` = length ls
 28     - `pattern.nmvars p` = length es
 29  
 30     The tactic fails if o and the types of es do not contain all universes ls and expressions es. -/
 31  meta constant mk_pattern : list level → list expr → expr → list level → list expr → tactic pattern
