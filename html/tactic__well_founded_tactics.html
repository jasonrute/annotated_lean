  8  Replacement for the definition of `well_founded_tactics.default_dec_tac` in core.
  9  -/
 10  
 11  namespace well_founded_tactics
 12  open tactic
 13  
 14  /--
 15  The definition of `default_dec_tac` in core is broken, because `unfold_sizeof`
 16  could actually discharge the goal.
 17  
 18  Here we add a test using `done` to detect this.
 19  -/

 21  abstract $

 22  do clear_internals,
id      └─────────────┘

 26  
 27  /--
 28  The default `well_founded_tactics` provided in core are broken in some situations, often indicated
 29  by the message
 30  ```
 31  The nested exception contains the failure state for the decreasing tactic.
 32  nested exception message:
 33  tactic failed, there are no goals to be solved
 34  state:
 35  no goals
 36  ```
 37  
 38  Use this replacement by adding
 39  ```
 40  using_well_founded wf_tacs
 41  ```
 42  at the end of your inductive definition.
 43  -/
