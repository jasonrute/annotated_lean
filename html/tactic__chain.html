 13  /-
 14  This file defines a `chain` tactic, which takes a list of tactics, and exhaustively tries to apply them
 15  to the goals, until no tactic succeeds on any goal.
 16  
 17  Along the way, it generates auxiliary declarations, in order to speed up elaboration time
 18  of the resulting (sometimes long!) proofs.
 19  
 20  This tactic is used by the `tidy` tactic.
 21  -/
 22  
 23  -- α is the return type of our tactics. When `chain` is called by `tidy`, this is string,
 24  -- describing what that tactic did as an interactive tactic.
 25  variable {α : Type}
 26  
 27  /-
 28  Because chain sometimes pauses work on the first goal and works on later goals, we need a method
 29  for combining a list of results generated while working on a later goal into a single result.
 30  This enables `tidy {trace_result := tt}` to output faithfully reproduces its operation, e.g.
 31  ````
 32  intros,
 33  simp,
 34  apply lemma_1,
 35  work_on_goal 2 {
 36    dsimp,
 37    simp
 38  },
 39  refl
 40  ````
 41  -/
