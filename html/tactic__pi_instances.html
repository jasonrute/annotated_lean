 44  /--
 45  `pi_instance` constructs an instance of `my_class (Π i : I, f i)`
 46  where we know `Π i, my_class (f i)`. If an order relation is required,
 47  it defaults to `pi.partial_order`. Any field of the instance that
 48  `pi_instance` cannot construct is left untouched and generated as a new goal.
 49  -/
