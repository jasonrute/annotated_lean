  7  /-!
  8  # Recurrence Lemmas for the `continuants` Function of Continued Fractions.
  9  
 10  ## Summary
 11  
 12  Given a generalized continued fraction `g`, for all `n ≥ 1`, we prove that the `continuants`
 13  function indeed satisfies the following recurrences:
 14  
 15    `Aₙ = bₙ * Aₙ₋₁ + aₙ * Aₙ₋₂`, and `Bₙ = bₙ * Bₙ₋₁ + aₙ * Bₙ₋₂`.
 16  -/
