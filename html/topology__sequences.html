 10  /-!
 11  # Sequences in topological spaces
 12  
 13  In this file we define sequences in topological spaces and show how they are related to
 14  filters and the topology. In particular, we
 15  * associate a filter with a sequence and prove equivalence of convergence of the two,
 16  * define the sequential closure of a set and prove that it's contained in the closure,
 17  * define a type class "sequential_space" in which closure and sequential closure agree,
 18  * define sequential continuity and show that it coincides with continuity in sequential spaces,
 19  * provide an instance that shows that every first-countable (and in particular metric) space is a sequential space.
 20

 22  * Sequential compactness should be handled here.
 23  -/
