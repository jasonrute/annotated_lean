134  /--
135  Attempts to discharge goals in the quantifier-free fragment of
136  linear integer and natural number arithmetic using the Omega test.
137  Guesses the correct domain by looking at the goal and hypotheses,
138  and then reverts all relevant hypotheses and variables.
139  Use `omega manual` to disable automatic reverts, and `omega int` or
140  `omega nat` to specify the domain.
141  -/
