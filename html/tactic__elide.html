 53  /-- The `elide n (at ...)` tactic hides all subterms of the target goal or hypotheses
 54    beyond depth `n` by replacing them with `hidden`, which is a variant
 55    on the identity function. (Tactics should still mostly be able to see
 56    through the abbreviation, but if you want to unhide the term you can use
 57    `unelide`.) -/
