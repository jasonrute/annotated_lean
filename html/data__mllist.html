  6  Monadic lazy lists.
  7  
  8  The inductive construction is not allowed outside of meta (indeed, we can build infinite objects).
  9  This isn't so bad, as the typical use is with the tactic monad, in any case.
 10  
 11  As we're in meta anyway, we don't bother with proofs about these constructions.
