 10  # The Fibonacci Sequence
 11  
 12  ## Summary
 13  
 14  Definition of the Fibonacci sequence `F₀ = 0, F₁ = 1, Fₙ₊₂ = Fₙ + Fₙ₊₁`.
 15  
 16  ## Main Definitions
 17  
 18  - `fib` returns the stream of Fibonacci numbers.
 19  
 20  ## Main Statements
 21  
 22  - `fib_succ_succ` shows that `fib` indeed satisfies the Fibonacci recurrence `Fₙ₊₂ = Fₙ + Fₙ₊₁.`.
 23  
 24  ## Implementation Notes
 25  
 26  For efficiency purposes, the sequence is defined using `stream.iterate`.
 27  
 28  ## Tags
 29  
 30  fib, fibonacci
