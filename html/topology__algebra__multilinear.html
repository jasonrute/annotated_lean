 10  # Continuous multilinear maps
 11  
 12  We define continuous multilinear maps as maps from `Π(i : ι), M₁ i` to `M₂` which are multilinear
 13  and continuous, by extending the space of multilinear maps with a continuity assumption.
 14  Here, `M₁ i` and `M₂` are modules over a ring `R`, and `ι` is an arbitrary type, and all these
 15  spaces are also topological spaces.
 16  
 17  ## Main definitions
 18  
 19  * `continuous_multilinear_map R M₁ M₂` is the space of continuous multilinear maps from
 20    `Π(i : ι), M₁ i` to `M₂`. We show that it is an `R`-module.
 21  
 22  ## Implementation notes
 23  
 24  We mostly follow the API of multilinear maps.
 25  -/
