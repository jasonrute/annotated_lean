 10  /-
 11  * Meas, the category of measurable spaces
 12  
 13  Measurable spaces and measurable functions form a (concrete) category Meas.
 14  
 15  Measure : Meas ⥤ Meas is the functor which sends a measurable space X
 16  to the space of measures on X; it is a monad (the "Giry monad").
 17  
 18  Borel : Top ⥤ Meas sends a topological space X to X equipped with the
 19  σ-algebra of Borel sets (the σ-algebra generated by the open subsets of X).
 20  
 21  ## Tags
 22  
 23  measurable space, giry monad, borel
 24  
 25  -/

 43  /-- `Measure X` is the measurable space of measures over the measurable space `X`. It is the
 44  weakest measurable space, s.t. λμ, μ s is measurable for all measurable sets `s` in `X`. An
 45  important purpose is to assign a monadic structure on it, the Giry monad. In the Giry monad,
 46  the pure values are the Dirac measure, and the bind operation maps to the integral:
 47  `(μ >>= ν) s = ∫ x. (ν x) s dμ`.
 48  
 49  In probability theory, the `Meas`-morphisms `X → Prob X` are (sub-)Markov kernels (here `Prob` is
 50  the restriction of `Measure` to (sub-)probability space.)
 51  -/
