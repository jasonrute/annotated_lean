  6  Defines natural transformations between functors.
  7  
  8  Introduces notations
  9    `τ.app X` for the components of natural transformations,
 10    `F ⟶ G` for the type of natural transformations between functors `F` and `G`,
 11    `σ ≫ τ` for vertical compositions, and
 12    `σ ◫ τ` for horizontal compositions.
 13  -/
