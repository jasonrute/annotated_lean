  9  inductive congr_arg_kind
 10  /- It is a parameter for the congruence lemma, the parameter occurs in the left and right hand sides. -/
 11  | fixed
 12  /- It is not a parameter for the congruence lemma, the lemma was specialized for this parameter.
 13     This only happens if the parameter is a subsingleton/proposition, and other parameters depend on it. -/
 14  | fixed_no_param
 15  /- The lemma contains three parameters for this kind of argument a_i, b_i and (eq_i : a_i = b_i).
 16     a_i and b_i represent the left and right hand sides, and eq_i is a proof for their equality. -/
 17  | eq
 18  /- congr-simp lemma contains only one parameter for this kind of argument, and congr-lemmas contains two.
 19     They correspond to arguments that are subsingletons/propositions. -/
 20  | cast
 21  /- The lemma contains three parameters for this kind of argument a_i, b_i and (eq_i : a_i == b_i).
 22     a_i and b_i represent the left and right hand sides, and eq_i is a proof for their heterogeneous equality. -/
 23  | heq
