 10  # The extended reals [-∞, ∞].
 11  
 12  This file defines `ereal`, the real numbers together with a top and bottom element,
 13  referred to as ⊤ and ⊥. It is implemented as `with_top (with_bot ℝ)`
 14  
 15  Addition and multiplication are problematic in the presence of ±∞, but
 16  negation has a natural definition and satisfies the usual properties.
 17  An addition is derived, but `ereal` is not even a monoid (there is no identity).
 18  
 19  `ereal` is a `complete_lattice`; this is now deduced by type class inference from
 20  the fact that `with_top (with_bot L)` is a complete lattice if `L` is
 21  a conditionally complete lattice.
 22  
 23  ## Tags
 24  
 25  real, ereal, complete lattice
