 71  /-- Close goals of the form `n ≠ m` when `n` and `m` have type `nat`, `char`, `string`, `int` or `fin sz`,
 72      and they are literals. It also closes goals of the form `n < m`, `n > m`, `n ≤ m` and `n ≥ m` for `nat`.
 73      If the foal is of the form `n = m`, then it tries to close it using reflexivity. -/
