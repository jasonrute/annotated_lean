 10  # Conjugate morphisms by isomorphisms
 11  
 12  An isomorphism `α : X ≅ Y` defines
 13  - a monoid isomorphism `conj : End X ≃* End Y` by `α.conj f = α.inv ≫ f ≫ α.hom`;
 14  - a group isomorphism `conj_Aut : Aut X ≃* Aut Y` by `α.conj_Aut f = α.symm ≪≫ f ≪≫ α`.
 15  
 16  For completeness, we also define `hom_congr : (X ≅ X₁) → (Y ≅ Y₁) → (X ⟶ Y) ≃ (X₁ ⟶ Y₁)`, cf. `equiv.arrow_congr`.
 17  -/
