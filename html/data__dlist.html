  7  /--
  8  A difference list is a function that, given a list, returns the original
  9  contents of the difference list prepended to the given list.
 10  
 11  This structure supports `O(1)` `append` and `concat` operations on lists, making it
 12  useful for append-heavy uses such as logging and pretty printing.
 13  -/
