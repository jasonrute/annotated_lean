 11  # Binary (co)products
 12  
 13  We define a category `walking_pair`, which is the index category
 14  for a binary (co)product diagram. A convenience method `pair X Y`
 15  constructs the functor from the walking pair, hitting the given objects.
 16  
 17  We define `prod X Y` and `coprod X Y` as limits and colimits of such functors.
 18  
 19  Typeclasses `has_binary_products` and `has_binary_coproducts` assert the existence
 20  of (co)limits shaped as walking pairs.
 21  -/
